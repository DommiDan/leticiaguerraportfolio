Juniors

Juniors is a Family-Friendly fast-food place. Families can play numerous games and activities while they wait for their food. Our goal is to bring families together

The Research

I did a lot of research on fast food restaurants like McDonalds, Burger King, Friendly's & Wendy's and how their websites look and how they make it easier for users to follow. They all had a pattern of using visuals and very simple design to make it easier for people to use. They very rarely rely on a lot of text and their font sizes are very big. I also looked at a lot of pintrest UX designs that focuses on restaurant layout design.
