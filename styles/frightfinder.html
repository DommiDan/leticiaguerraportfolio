Fright Finder

Fright Finder is a collaborative project I've done with a group of 3 developers. Our goal was to make an app that finds 9000+ haunted places closest to you and Google Maps looks for the location for you and it populates an image of where that haunted place would be. This website also comes with eerie audio that makes your spine tingle when you open the app, with eerie and visuals, hidden foot steps and an abandoned fire place.
