Seasons Weather App

Seasons Weather App is a graphic-designed weather app that gives you real time weather changes while also having a stylized twist!

The Research

This Project was inspired by another weather app I created with my group called Traffic JamThis one is more graphic design based compared to "Traffic Jam" It has different colors and fonts and less white space. I wanted to make it look more stylish, something that could attract someones attention and make them wanna use the app. I've looked at similar things to what other weather apps do and they are all simplistic and straight to the point to I wanted to implement that into the design and the wire framing. The Simplicity makes it easier to use and the visuals make it engaging.
