Albumy

This is an all around easy to use music application that shows top favorite songs, and song choices and also gives you a chance to look at music videos, which not many music applications do.

The Research

We listen to music apps all the time, So I wanted to make a simplistic music app called "Albumy". A lot of the functionality was inspired by music apps like "Spotify". It was a nice dark background with simple buttons and is easy to navigate.
